FOCUS Adelaide Uni

Start-of-year Camps

Heaps of fun and a great way to meet other students

Whether you're new to uni or a continuing student, our camps are a fantastic way to kick-off the year! Run by Evangelical Students, a part of the Australian Fellowship of Evangelical Students, the camp provides you with time for making friends, to find out more about uni life, and think about what God has to say about it all.
ES is a Christian student group, but you don't have to be a Christian to come on the camp. We encourage you to join us wherever you are at with God.
